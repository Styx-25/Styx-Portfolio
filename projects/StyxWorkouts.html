Supplement Guide

Pre-Workout:

Take 20-30 minutes before your workout for an energy boost and improved focus. Don't take it too late in the day to avoid sleep issues.

Creatine:

Take 5g daily. Timing isn't critical, but many prefer taking it post-workout with their protein shake for better absorption. Consistency is key.

Protein Powder/Shake:

Best consumed within 60 minutes post-workout to aid muscle repair. Can also be taken throughout the day to meet your daily protein goals.

Day 6 & 7: Active Rest

Listen to your body. Light cardio, stretching, or a walk is recommended to aid recovery.
